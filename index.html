  1. Examine each symbol in the postfix formula, starting at extreme left until you come to an operator.
  2. Write down the operator and two operands immediately to its left on a piece of scratch paper.
  3. Erase the operator and the operands and write the result creating the hole .
  4. Perform the operation on the operands and write the result in the hole.
  5. If the formula now consists of the one value that means the algorithm is finished , else go to step 1.
